What Information We Collect From You?


When you create your account on the Website the only personal data we collect is your email address.

Additionally, we collect anonymous data related to the type of browser, browser version and operating system that you use. Such data shall not enable us to identify you and shall be gathered in a database for the purpose of being used for enhancing the services. For example which browsers or operating systems we need to develop additional features or functions.

The Website does not use any 1st party cookies. There are 3rd party cookies (for example, for Google analytics) that are used for statistical and analytical purposes. These 3rd party cookies are not linked in anyway to your personal data.

All the information collected from you is hereinafter referred to as the "Data".

Our Website is not dedicated or intended for people under 18 years of age. If you are under 18 years old, please do not sign up on our Website and do not use our Services. If we have good reasons to believe that you are under 18 years old we will take all necessary measures to deny you access to our Services. If you are a parent of a child and you know that he/she has accessed and uses our Website please contact us at privacy@proxmate.com.

We will process the Data in order to improve and develop our Services and the Website. The Data may also be used for legal compliance, such as fulfilling legal requirements in case of liability, to investigate or to defend us from third parties' claims, to support legal investigations of public authorities, to protect your rights and personal security. We will also use your email addresses in order to inform you with respect to any changes to the Website or other relevant similar information related to the Services.

By choosing to unsubscribe we assure you that from the moment we receive your choice to unsubscribe we shall limit the emails we send you to those necessary for fulfilling the purposes of processing mentioned above.

We do not collect any personal data which is not relevant to our scope of processing and we make sure that the Data is limited to the minimum necessary information to operate the Services. We do not use your Data for any other purposes other the ones described in this Privacy Policy except for when you have expressly consented to such processing.

We assure you that we do not store your Data for a period of time longer than necessary for fulfilling the purposes for which we process your Data. The servers where we store your Data are located in United Kingdom. We declare and guarantee that we have implemented all technical and organizational measures in order to ensure the integrity and security of your Data against any loss, destruction, theft or unauthorized access, use or modification.
